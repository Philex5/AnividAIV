# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** November 9, 2025
**Last Updated:** November 9, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **AnividAI ** (https://anividai.com), an AI-powered anime art generation platform that empowers you to create original characters (OCs), generate stunning anime images and videos, and engage with a creative community.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our services. By using AnividAI , you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

### 2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

- **Email address**
- **Username** (if provided)
- **Authentication provider information** (Google, GitHub, or email/password credentials)
- **Profile information** (display name, avatar, bio)
- **Account preferences and settings**

### 2.2 Original Character (OC) Data

When you use our OC Maker to create characters, we collect:

- **Character profiles**: Name, gender, age, personality traits, background story, hobbies
- **Character appearance**: Physical descriptions, clothing style, distinctive features
- **Character images**: Avatar images, generated character artwork
- **Visibility settings**: Public or private status for each character
- **Creation and modification timestamps**

### 2.3 AI-Generated Content

When you use our AI generation features, we collect:

- **Input data**: Text prompts, selected parameters, reference images
- **Generated content**: AI-created images, videos, and chat responses
- **Generation metadata**: Model used, parameters, generation timestamps
- **Content visibility settings**: Public or private status
- **Community interactions**: Likes, favorites, shares on public content

### 2.4 Chat and Communication Data

When you use the "Chat with OCs" feature, we collect:

- **Chat messages**: Your input messages and AI-generated responses
- **Chat sessions**: Session IDs, timestamps, character context
- **Chat history**: Stored conversations (subject to retention limits)

### 2.5 Meow Coin (MC) Transaction Data

To manage our credits system, we collect:

- **MC balance and transaction history**
- **Credit purchases and consumption records**
- **Earned credits** (new user bonuses, daily check-ins, referrals)
- **Credit expiration dates**
- **Membership subscription status and history**

### 2.6 Payment and Billing Information

When you purchase MC packs or subscriptions, we collect:

- **Payment method information** (processed securely by Stripe or Creem)
- **Billing address**
- **Purchase history and receipts**
- **Subscription plan details** (Free, Basic, Plus, Pro)
- **Subscription cancellation and refund requests**
- **Refund transaction records** (for annual subscriptions with partial refunds)

**Refund Data Collection (Annual Subscriptions):**

When you cancel an annual subscription and request a refund, we process:

- **Credit activation history**: Which monthly credit batches have been activated
- **Refund calculation details**: Number of activated vs. unactivated months
- **Refund amount**: Calculated based on unactivated monthly cycles
- **Credit voiding records**: Unactivated credits marked as voided to prevent future use
- **Cancellation reason**: Optional feedback you provide when canceling

This data is used to:
- Calculate accurate refund amounts based on our monthly billing cycle policy
- Prevent misuse of unactivated credits after refund processing
- Maintain financial records for accounting and compliance
- Improve our services based on cancellation feedback

**Note:** We do not store your full credit card numbers. Payment processing is handled by our trusted payment partners (Stripe and Creem) in compliance with PCI-DSS standards.

### 2.7 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you use our services:

- **Feature usage**: Which generation tools you use, models selected, parameters chosen
- **Generation history**: Number and types of generations performed
- **Community activity**: Content viewed, searched, and interacted with
- **Session data**: Login times, session duration, pages visited
- **Performance data**: Loading times, errors encountered

### 2.8 Device and Technical Data

We collect technical information about your device and connection:

- **Device information**: Device type, operating system, browser type and version
- **IP address and location data** (country/city level, not precise geolocation)
- **Browser cookies and similar tracking technologies**
- **Referral source**: How you found our website
- **Screen resolution and display settings**

### 2.9 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

- **Session management**: Keep you logged in and maintain preferences
- **Analytics**: Understand how users interact with our platform (using tools like Google Analytics)
- **Performance optimization**: Improve loading speeds and user experience
- **Security**: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Note that disabling certain cookies may limit your ability to use some features.

### 2.10 Cookie Consent and Your Choices

**Your Consent**: When you first visit our website, we will ask for your consent before using non-essential cookies (such as analytics cookies). You can:

- **Accept all cookies**: Allow us to use all types of cookies
- **Reject non-essential cookies**: Only necessary cookies will be used
- **Customize your preferences**: Choose which types of cookies to allow

**Managing Your Preferences**:
- You can change your cookie preferences at any time by visiting our [Cookie Settings](/cookie-settings) page
- You can also manage cookies through your browser settings
- If you disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly
- **Important**: We will not send your cookie preferences to our servers or any third parties - they are stored locally in your browser only

**Cookie Categories**:
1. **Necessary Cookies**: Always active - required for basic site functionality, security, and authentication. Including session management, login authentication, and security protection.
2. **Analytics Cookies**: Optional - Used to track page views and user behavior, helping us optimize website experience (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, Plausible). These cookies collect and report information anonymously.
3. **Marketing Cookies**: Optional - used for personalized advertising (not currently used)

**Withdrawing Consent**: You can withdraw your consent for non-essential cookies at any time by:
- Visiting our [Cookie Settings](/cookie-settings) page and adjusting your preferences
- Clearing cookies in your browser settings
- Contacting us at support@anividai.com

**Immediate Effect**: After withdrawing consent, relevant cookies will be immediately deleted and analytics scripts will stop loading.

**Data Retention**: We keep records of your cookie consent for up to 12 months or until you change your preferences. Your current cookie preferences are stored locally in your browser.

**For More Information**: Please refer to our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy) for complete details about how we handle your data and protect your privacy.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where We Store Your Data

- **Structured data** (accounts, characters, transactions): Stored in secure PostgreSQL databases
- **Generated media files** (images, videos): Stored in AWS S3 or Cloudflare R2 with encryption
- **Backup systems**: Regular automated backups for data recovery

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption**: HTTPS/TLS for data transmission, encryption at rest for sensitive data
- **Access controls**: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication for admin accounts
- **Security monitoring**: Continuous monitoring for suspicious activity
- **Regular audits**: Security assessments and vulnerability scanning
- **Data minimization**: We only collect data necessary for service provision

### 4.3 Data Retention

- **Account data**: Retained while your account is active
- **Generated content**: Retained based on your visibility settings and account status
- **Chat history**: Retained for 90 days or until you delete your account
- **Transaction records**: Retained for 7 years for accounting and legal compliance
- **Deleted data**: Permanently removed within 30 days after deletion request
