# AnividAI Series Blog Content Plan

Based on the [3 Layer Blog Theory](/posts/3_layer_blog_theory) and the [Ultimate Guide to AnividAI](/posts/anividai-series/ultimate_guide_to_anividai/ultimate_guide_to_anividai), this document outlines the content strategy for the AnividAI blog series. The goal is to capture traffic at different stages of the user journey, from awareness to conversion.

---

## I. TOFU (Top of Funnel) - Awareness & Cognition
**Goal:** Education, capturing broad search traffic, establishing trust.
**Focus:** High-volume keywords, beginner questions, industry trends.

### 1. Definitions & Beginner's Guides
*   **Title:** **What is an OC (Original Character)? The Complete Guide for Anime Fans**
    *   *Intent:* Define "OC", explain its cultural significance in anime/manga fandoms.
    *   *Keywords:* What is an OC, Original Character meaning, creating anime characters.
*   **Title:** **AI Anime Art 101: How Text-to-Image Generation Works**
    *   *Intent:* Demystify the tech for beginners without getting too technical.
    *   *Keywords:* AI anime art, how AI art works, text-to-image anime.
*   **Title:** **The Ultimate Glossary of Anime Art Prompts: Danbooru Tags & Terminology**
    *   *Intent:* Rank for specific prompt terms (e.g., "Ghibli style", "Cyberpunk", "Chibi").
    *   *Keywords:* Anime prompt guide, AI art tags, stable diffusion prompts anime.

### 2. Lists & Best-Ofs
*   **Title:** **Top 10 AI Tools for Creating Anime Characters in 2025**
    *   *Intent:* Capture "best ai anime generator" traffic. Mentions AnividAI alongside others but highlights unique OC focus.
    *   *Keywords:* Best AI anime generator, free anime art maker, character design AI.
*   **Title:** **50+ Best Anime Art Prompts to Try Right Now**
    *   *Intent:* Provide immediate value to users looking for inspiration.
    *   *Keywords:* Best anime prompts, cool AI art ideas, anime character prompts.

### 3. Pillar Page (The Hub)
*   **Title:** **The Ultimate Guide to AnividAI: Create, Chat, and Animate Your OCs**
    *   *Ref:* [Existing Guide](/posts/anividai-series/ultimate_guide_to_anividai/ultimate_guide_to_anividai)
    *   *Intent:* The central resource linking to all specific features.
    *   *Keywords:* AnividAI guide, how to use AnividAI, AI anime platform.

---

## II. MOFU (Middle of Funnel) - Evaluation
**Goal:** Guide "problem-aware" users to AnividAI as the solution.
**Focus:** Specific "How-to" workflows, comparisons, demonstrating capability.

### 1. How-To Guides (Problem Solving)
*   **Title:** **How to Maintain Character Consistency in AI Art (The "Same Face" Guide)**
    *   *Intent:* Solve the #1 pain point of AI art. Show how AnividAI's OC Maker solves this.
    *   *Keywords:* Consistent AI character, keep same face AI, AnividAI character consistency.
*   **Title:** **Step-by-Step: Turning Your Text Description into a 3D Anime Action Figure**
    *   *Intent:* showcase the specific "Action Figure Generator" feature.
    *   *Keywords:* AI action figure generator, anime figure design, custom anime figures.
*   **Title:** **How to Create Your Own Anime Sticker Pack for Telegram/Discord in Minutes**
    *   *Intent:* Practical application of the "Sticker Generator".
    *   *Keywords:* AI sticker maker, custom anime stickers, discord sticker generator.
*   **Title:** **From Static Image to Viral Video: Animating Your OC with AI**
    *   *Intent:* Guide for the Video Generator feature.
    *   *Keywords:* AI anime video generator, animate image AI, image to video anime.

### 2. Comparisons & Alternatives
*   **Title:** **AnividAI vs. Midjourney vs. NovelAI: Which is Best for Anime Creators?**
    *   *Intent:* Help users deciding between platforms. Highlight AnividAI's "All-in-one" & "OC-centric" advantages.
    *   *Keywords:* AnividAI vs Midjourney, NovelAI alternative, best anime AI tool comparison.
*   **Title:** **Why You Need a Specialized "OC Maker" Instead of General AI Generators**
    *   *Intent:* Differentiate the product philosophy (Character-centric vs. Image-centric).
    *   *Keywords:* OC maker vs AI generator, character design tool.

### 3. Case Studies / Templates
*   **Title:** **Showcase: 5 Incredible OCs Created entirely with AnividAI**
    *   *Intent:* Social proof. Show prompt + result + OC card.
    *   *Keywords:* AnividAI examples, AI OC showcase, best AI character designs.

---

## III. BOFU (Bottom of Funnel) - Conversion
**Goal:** Closing the deal, transactional queries.
**Focus:** Pricing value, specific feature deep-dives, trust building.

### 1. Product & Pricing Focus
*   **Title:** **Is AnividAI Worth It? A Breakdown of Free vs. Pro Plans**
    *   *Intent:* Overcome price objections. Explain the value of "Meow Coins" and unlimited OCs.
    *   *Keywords:* AnividAI pricing, AnividAI free trial, AnividAI cost.
*   **Title:** **Commercial Use & Copyright: Can I Sell My AnividAI Art?**
    *   *Intent:* Address a critical objection for professional creators.
    *   *Keywords:* AnividAI copyright, sell AI art, commercial license AI anime.

### 2. Feature Deep Dives (Landing Page Style)
*   **Title:** **Feature Spotlight: Chat with OC - Immersive Roleplay Explained**
    *   *Intent:* Sell the "Chat" feature specifically to roleplayers.
    *   *Keywords:* AI character chat, roleplay with OC, anime chatbot.
*   **Title:** **For Developers & Power Users: Understanding AnividAI's Model Options (Kling, Wan2.5, etc.)**
    *   *Intent:* Appeal to technical users who care about the underlying models.
    *   *Keywords:* AnividAI models, Kling video AI, Wan2.5 anime.

---

## Execution Plan Summary

1.  **Phase 1 (Foundation):** Polish the **Pillar Page** (Ultimate Guide) and publish 2 **Definitions** (TOFU) to establish context.
2.  **Phase 2 (Traffic):** Release **"Top 10 Tools"** (TOFU) and **"Consistency Guide"** (MOFU) to catch search traffic and fix user pain points.
3.  **Phase 3 (Conversion):** Publish **"Pricing Breakdown"** (BOFU) and specific **Feature Guides** (MOFU) linked from the Pillar Page.
4.  **Ongoing:** Weekly **Showcase** (MOFU) and new **Prompt Lists** (TOFU) to keep content fresh.
